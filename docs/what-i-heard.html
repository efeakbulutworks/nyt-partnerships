Section 01 · The New York Times Advertising Partnerships

What I Heard

"The challenge is not demand generation."
"The challenge is operationalizing high-value bespoke partnerships across NYT premium brands without diluting creativity, overwhelming the team, or compromising long-term pipeline development."

NYT has no shortage of opportunity. The harder question is which opportunities deserve organizational energy, how to scale the team's output thoughtfully, and how to build a 2027 pipeline while still executing 2026 — without losing the premium feel that makes NYT, NYT.

So the work isn't lead generation. It's prioritization, sequencing, and operating discipline. The shift I'd propose, and the spine of the rest of this site, is from reactive partnerships to portfolio management — managing the partnership book the way mature organizations manage an investment portfolio.

From reactive partnerships → portfolio management.
